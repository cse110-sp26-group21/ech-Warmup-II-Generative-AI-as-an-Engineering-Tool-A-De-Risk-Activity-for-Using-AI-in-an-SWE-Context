# Serina Sato 🎮✨💸
![alt text](serina.png)

**Details**: Serina Sato is a 19-year-old college student majoring in Graphic Design who streams video games on Twitch. She recently started playing casino games because they bring in higher viewership and give her the chance to earn extra money from winnings. Her favorite games are slot machines because of their fun themes, animations, and visual design. Lately, however, she has grown bored with her usual rotation of slot games and is looking for something more entertaining, fresh, and visually appealing. She also wishes there were more ways to customize the experience, such as changing characters, music, themes, and other design elements. Serina is highly prone to making in-app purchases, especially when they enhance gameplay or aesthetics.

**Wants:**
- New and exciting slot game experiences
- Strong visual design, animations, and creative themes
- Customization options (characters, music, skins, backgrounds, effects)
- Games that attract viewers and keep streams entertaining
- Opportunities for rewards or big wins
- Frequent content updates and limited-time events
- Social or stream-friendly features (sharing wins, adding friends)

**Needs**
- A game that stays engaging over time without becoming repetitive
- Clear and fair reward systems
- Easy-to-use interface with polished visuals
- Budget controls or spending limits for in-app purchases
- Variety in gameplay mechanics beyond standard slots
- Reliable performance while streaming
  
**Doesn’t Want**
- Repetitive slot games with the same mechanics
- Bland or outdated visuals / boring to watch 
- Limited customization options
- Slow gameplay or long wait times between spins
- Cluttered or confusing menus
- Frequent crashes, lag, or technical issues
- Aggressive pop-ups that interrupt gameplay

**Doesn’t Need**
- Overly complicated rules or mechanics
- Text-heavy interface
- Realistic casino simulations with no creative style
- Too many currencies or confusing reward systems
- Expensive purchases required to enjoy the game
- Generic themes that all look the same
